# Enterprise Mobile Workforce & Manager Platform Specification

Design the complete Enterprise Mobile Workforce & Manager Platform for the Enterprise Fashion ERP/POS system.

This module provides production-ready mobile experiences for managers, executives, supervisors, owners, and operational staff. It enables real-time monitoring, approvals, workforce management, inventory visibility, sales tracking, notifications, business intelligence, and remote operations.

The platform integrates with every ERP module including POS, Product Management, Inventory, Purchase, Sales, Customers, Loyalty, Promotions, CRM, Suppliers, Finance, Reporting, Employees, Multi-Branch, Notifications, Barcode, E-commerce, AI Analytics, Role & Permission, and API Integration.
