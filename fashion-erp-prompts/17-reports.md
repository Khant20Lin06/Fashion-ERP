# Enterprise Fashion ERP/POS
# Business Intelligence (BI), Reporting & Analytics Platform Specification

Design the complete Business Intelligence (BI), Reporting & Analytics platform for the Enterprise Fashion ERP/POS system.

This module provides enterprise reporting, KPI monitoring, operational dashboards, executive analytics, financial reporting, inventory analytics, customer insights, and AI-powered business intelligence across all modules.

The platform integrates with POS, Product Management, Inventory, Purchase, Sales, Customers, Loyalty, Promotions, Marketing Automation, CRM, Suppliers, Finance, Employees, Multi-Branch, and E-commerce.
